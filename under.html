Information on the Under

Depending on the perception of the person in The Under it could be a beautiful wonderland, or a torturous hellscape. Due to Reiz being a tour guide he can perceive all versions of The Under, including its true form, which is a gigantic empty cavern. In that version there is a low, but piercing groan which permeates the whole layer constantly.The sound comes from the huge amount of spirits gathered in The Under, even those without normal auditory function can hear it.

Reiz himself spends most of his time on touring routes, but when he has the free time, he spends it alone in his personal version of The Under, which takes the form of his old college dorm when he lived.
