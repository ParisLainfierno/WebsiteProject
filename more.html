More about Reiz

Normally, Reiz is rather average in height and build of a human, his body has a faint electric glow to it. He can float, fly, and pass through physical objects.

Reiz is somewhat disinterested and bored with his time as a spirit and a Under tour guide, he carries himself very nonchalantly and lazily. Having a long time both dead and as a tour guide, he has gotten quite cynical towards the idea of the afterlife.
